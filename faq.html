Residential Locksmith Services

Rekeying is recommended when:

  • Moving into a new home
  • Lost keys or stolen keys
  • Former tenant/employee had access
  • Locks are in good condition

Replacement is better when:

  • Locks are old or damaged
  • Upgrading security level
  • Installing smart locks
  • Changing lock types or styles

Our technicians will assess your situation and recommend the most cost-effective solution.

We install and service all types of residential locks:

  • Deadbolts: Single and double cylinder deadbolts
  • Smart Locks: Keypad, Bluetooth, and WiFi-enabled locks
  • Handle Sets: Entry and privacy handle sets
  • High-Security Locks: Medeco, Mul-T-Lock, and other premium brands
  • Electronic Locks: Keyless entry systems
  • Sliding Door Locks: Patio and sliding glass door security

Installation times vary by lock type:

  • Standard Deadbolt: 15-30 minutes
  • Handle Set: 20-40 minutes
  • Smart Lock: 30-60 minutes (includes setup and programming)
  • High-Security Lock: 30-45 minutes
  • Complete Re-keying: 10-15 minutes per lock

Complex installations or modifications may take longer. Our technician will provide a time estimate before starting work.

Automotive Locksmith Services

Yes, we can create keys for virtually all vehicle makes and models, including:

  • Domestic Vehicles: Ford, Chevrolet, Chrysler, Cadillac, etc.
  • Import Vehicles: Honda, Toyota, BMW, Mercedes, Audi, etc.
  • Luxury Vehicles: Lexus, Infiniti, Acura, Jaguar, etc.
  • Specialty Vehicles: Motorcycles, RVs, commercial vehicles

Our advanced equipment can handle traditional keys, transponder keys, smart keys, and key fobs for vehicles from the 1990s to current models.

Regular Keys: Basic metal keys that physically operate locks and ignition (common in pre-1995 vehicles).

Transponder Keys: Contains a computer chip that communicates with your vehicle's security system. Benefits include:

  • Enhanced anti-theft protection
  • Engine immobilizer system
  • Required for most vehicles 1995 and newer
  • Must be programmed to your specific vehicle

We can cut and program both types, often on-site at your location.

Programming times vary by vehicle:

  • Basic Transponder: 10-20 minutes
  • Smart Key/Proximity: 20-45 minutes
  • Key Fob: 15-30 minutes
  • Push-to-Start Systems: 30-60 minutes

Luxury and European vehicles may require additional time. Our technician will provide an accurate time estimate based on your specific vehicle and key type.

  • We use OEM-equivalent keys and programming procedures

Key replacement is considered routine maintenance and is explicitly allowed under federal warranty laws.

  • Master Key: Building owners/administrators access all areas

Benefits include improved security, access control, and reduced key management complexity.

Security & Technology

Consider upgrading your locks if you notice:

  • Difficulty turning keys or operating locks
  • Keys getting stuck or breaking
  • Visible rust, corrosion, or damage
  • Locks are more than 7-10 years old
  • Recent break-in attempts in your area
  • Moving into a new home or office
  • Wanting to upgrade to smart lock technology

We provide free security assessments to evaluate your current locks and recommend appropriate upgrades.

High-security locks offer superior protection through:

  • Pick Resistance: Advanced pin systems prevent lock picking
  • Drill Resistance: Hardened steel components resist drilling attacks
  • Key Control: Patented key systems prevent unauthorized duplication
  • Grade 1 Rating: Highest ANSI security rating

Recommended for: High-crime areas, valuable property, businesses, front doors, and anyone wanting maximum security.

Modern smart locks offer excellent security when properly installed:

Security Features:

  • 128-bit encryption for wireless communication
  • Physical backup key options
  • Activity logs and access monitoring
  • Auto-locking capabilities

Reliability Considerations:

  • Battery life typically 6-12 months
  • Weather-resistant for outdoor use
  • Professional installation ensures proper operation

We recommend reputable brands and provide professional installation and setup.
