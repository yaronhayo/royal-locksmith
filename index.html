Everything you need to know about our locksmith services

For exterior doors, we recommend high-security deadbolts (ANSI Grade 1) for maximum protection. Popular brands we work with include Schlage, Kwikset, and Medeco. For enhanced security, consider smart locks with keyless entry options or electronic keypad locks. During a consultation, we can recommend specific products based on your security needs.

If you're locked out of your house, the first thing you should do is try to find a spare key or call a trusted neighbor or friend for assistance. If that's not possible, you can call Royal Locksmith for a quick and reliable solution. Our professional locksmiths are trained to handle various lockout situations efficiently and safely.

Rekeying involves changing the internal pins of a lock so it works with a new key, while the original hardware remains in place. Replacing locks means installing entirely new locking hardware. We recommend rekeying when your existing locks are in good condition but you want to change who has access. Full replacement is better when locks are damaged, outdated, or you want to upgrade security.

Yes, we can make new car keys even if you've lost all your original keys. This service works for most vehicle makes and models, including those with transponder chips, proximity keys, or smart keys. The process typically involves verifying your ownership of the vehicle (through registration and ID), creating a new key, and programming it to communicate with your vehicle's immobilizer system.

Yes, we specialize in designing and implementing commercial master key systems for businesses of all sizes. Our master key systems allow different levels of access for various personnel, with master keys that can open all doors and individual keys that only open specific areas. This creates a hierarchical security system that balances convenience with security control. We can create simple systems or complex multi-level systems based on your business needs.

Yes, all our locksmiths are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We operate under NJ DCA License #13VH13550300. Our technicians undergo rigorous background checks and continuous training to ensure they provide the highest level of professional service. Being fully insured means that you're protected in the unlikely event of any damages during service, giving you complete peace of mind when using our locksmith services.
